# Final Product Vision

**Vakil Sirji Property Manager**

A Flutter-based Web + Android platform where:

- Existing customers access their registered agreements.
- New customers manage properties and rent.
- Agreement expiry automatically generates renewal leads.
- Vakil Sirji processes Maharashtra Leave & License registrations through the same system.
- Vendors handle doorstep biometric appointments.
- Owners, tenants, vendors, and staff work from one unified platform.

---

This vision outlines the unified experience for all stakeholders, streamlining property management, legal processes, and service appointments in a single, cohesive application.
